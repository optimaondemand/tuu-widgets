About Independence: Each roll of a die is completely independent of all previous rolls. The die has no memory! Even if you roll five 6s in a row, the next roll still has a 1-in-6 chance of being a 6. The bar chart above shows that over many rolls, each face appears roughly equally often — exactly what independence predicts.
