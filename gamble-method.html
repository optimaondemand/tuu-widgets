Gamble Method: Measuring Your Utility

Ramsey's technique — finding your personal utility curve

Introduction

What is utility?

Money doesn't always feel proportional. Going from $0 to $25 might feel huge, but $75 to $100 might feel less exciting. Utility captures how much you actually value different outcomes — on a scale from 0 to 1.

We'll anchor: U($0) = 0 and U($100) = 1.
Then we'll measure U($25), U($50), and U($75) using a clever gamble technique.

For each amount, you'll compare a sure thing vs a risky gamble. Your answer adjusts the gamble's odds until you're indifferent — that probability is your utility value.
